Our Story

  • 3CS Launches New Website for Kia Motors Sri Lanka

    KIA Motors together with Carplan Limited introduced the KIA brand to Sri Lanka in 1996 providing sales and services to the brand for the past 21 years. Gaining customer confidence in the Sri Lankan market, today KIA is rapidly moving forward on the strength of a “DESIGN” driven brand and recognized as a quality product. KIA’s design-driven aesthetics and performance and value-for-money pricing are the principal factors behind the brand’s popularity. The brand has lived up to the pledge made by Peter Schreyer when he took over as Design Chief of KIA, that he would create design-driven vehicles with all the advances of technology, performance, features and comfort without the attendant high price of other design-driven brands. The website was designed in a modern user friendly style and layout. It’s an interactive site, which gives visitors useful information about the products, services and facilities available at KIA Motors Sri Lanka. The site is fully responsive and compatible with all mobile devices and platforms. As with every 3CS solution, this website runs on state of the art infrastructure, and has been highly optimized for search engines.

    3CS Wins Best Finance Website Award for LOLC Finance Website

    LOLC Finance PLC (www.lolcfinance.com) was awarded the Best Website in the Finance category at the SLT ZERO ONE Digital Excellence Awards 2018. The website was designed by 3CS (www.3cs.lk). The award ceremony was held at the Galadari Hotel on 11th December 2018. 3CS also won two awards as Best Digital Media Agency in the Finance and Travel categories.

    3CS won a Best Web Developer award at Bestweb for a record 7th consecutive time. The News1st website designed and managed by 3CS won the Gold Award in the Media, Sports & Entertainment category, and also won the Gold Award for Best Overall Website in Sri Lanka. 3CS designed websites for Renuka City Hotel, John Keells Foundation and Ceylon Escapes also won awards in other categories. 3CS continued to dominate the Travel & Tourism category, winning 2/3 awards handed out, including the highest award in the category.

  • May 2018

    3CS Launches New Website for LOLC Finance

    LOLC Finance is the largest Non-Bank Financial Institution in Sri Lanka, specialising in a wide array of financial services such as Savings and Fixed Deposits, Leasing, Islamic Banking, Micro Leasing, Loans and Cards. LOLC Finance has many years of experience in the financial sector.The expertise they have amassed in the industry helps them to provide their customers with an unparalleled set of services. LOLC Finance is a subsidiary of the LOLC Group, which has a history of 34 years and is a vibrant and fast growing conglomerate involved in many sectors. The redesign of the LOLC Finance website was timed to coincide with the merger of LOLC Finance with LOLC Micro Credit. With a focus on user-friendliness, the new LOLC Finance site is simple and easy to navigate. It is mobile responsive and compatible with all devices, browsers and platforms. The design 3CS presented for LOLC Finance reflects our evolving design language and philosophy. The site offers many interactive features including a branch locator, rate calculators and access to online portals and the mobile app. The previous design of the LOLC website was also done by 3CS several years ago. 3CS has also designed and maintains several other websites for the LOLC group, including the main corporate website, LOLC Life, LOLC General, LOLC Motors and CLC. We are proud to continue being a web design partner for the LOLC Group.
